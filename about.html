Nice to meet you!

Let's get to know each other a little bit.


Hi, I'm Paige. I love coding, I love designing, and I love the internet! At age 22, I know I have a lot of great things ahead of me. I've been lucky enough to work with some great companies and be apart of some amazing projects, including completing a coding challenge with Cornell's Women in Technology and Entrepreneurship and helping to design Waave, a new e-hailing app focused on local New York taxi drivers.

Ever since I was a child, I've been curious about how the internet works and who designs websites. Everyone uses the internet, but few people wonder about who made the website you're currently using or even how they made it. The digital age is a confusing yet mesmerizing place for everyone - including young people. I love teaching others the basics of coding and collaborating on projects.

While I love working by myself to crete and design things, I also love working on collabortive projects with friends and different team members. Web design is also a process, which is one reason why I love it so much -- there's so much room for creativity and growth. A website's aesthetic can start in one place, but end up in a totally different direction. After doing A/B testing, for example, we might realize that one layout or color palette makes for a better user experience than another. Getting input from a team of designers and developers makes the end result even better, even if there are some conflicts in opinion and speed bumps.
