Hi, My name is Paige!

I'm 22 years old, a recent Emerging Media graduate, and in love web design!

There's something almost magical about designing content, writing code, and watching it come to life on screen as something responsive and new. Media, design, and the web are my passions - that's why I studied Emerging Media at Hunter College and earned my BA in 2019. My work focuses on finding a balance between thinking outside of the box and following current design trends. I also love working with templates, putting my own flair on them, and turning them into something completely new and personal.

Whether it's web design, web development, or graphic design, I'm always up for the challenge of create something new and interesting. Using technologies like HTML5, CSS, JavaScript, and p5.js, I enjoy finding enw ways to express myself on the web. Just like the web, my style and capabilities are constantly evolving and expanding.
